Recommendations & Next Steps

Strategies to maximize results and for continuous optimization.

Creative Strategy

  • Prioritize authentic "before-and-after" photos and videos.
  • Create short videos showcasing the team, the shop, and customer testimonials to build trust.

Targeting Strategy (Meta Ads)

  • Hyperlocal: Focus ads within a 10-15 mile (15-25 km) radius of the shop.
  • Interests: Target users with interests in specific car brands (Tesla, BMW, Mercedes).
  • High-Value Audiences: Create "Lookalike Audiences" from your list of best customers.

Continuous Optimization

  • Phase 1 (Months 1-2): Launch campaigns and gather data to establish the account's actual CPL.
  • Phase 2 (Month 3+): Re-evaluate budgets based on performance. If Google Ads has a very low CPL, we can reallocate a portion of the Meta budget to scale what is already working.
